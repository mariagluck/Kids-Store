Sustainable Fabrics

H O W L MINI uses ecological, organic and recycled fabrics or materials, as well as textiles produced in small mills with traditional techniques. Sometimes traditional ways of producing fabrics tend to disappear we aim to support these production methods by integrating these materials into our designs and helping small communities with unique weaving techniques.

We use digital prints and organic dyeing when it comes to colors. Production is done at our studio, or at local/regional production companies, always sustainable, implementing all rights and labor standards.
